Faculty of Science

The Faculty of Science offers undergraduate and graduate programs in various scientific fields, including:

  • Department of Physics - Physics is the study of matter and energy, from the smallest subatomic particles to the largest structures in the universe.
  • Department of Chemistry - Chemistry is the study of matter and its properties, and how different substances interact with each other.
  • Department of Mathematics - Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, and shapes, and the relationships between them.

Faculty of Arts

The Faculty of Arts offers undergraduate and graduate programs in a variety of humanities and social science fields, including:

  • Department of History - History is the study of past events, societies, and cultures.
  • Department of English - English is the study of literature, language, and writing.
  • Department of Music - Music is the study of sound and its creation, performance, and significance.

Faculty of Engineering

The Faculty of Engineering offers undergraduate and graduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including:

  • Department of Mechanical Engineering - Mechanical engineering is the study of mechanical systems, machines, and energy conversion.
  • Department of Electrical Engineering - Electrical engineering is the study of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism.
  • Department of Civil Engineering - Civil engineering is the study of infrastructure, including buildings, bridges, roads, and water supply systems.
